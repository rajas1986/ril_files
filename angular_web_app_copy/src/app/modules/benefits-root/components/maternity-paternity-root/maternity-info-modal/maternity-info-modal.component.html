Maternity Leaves

STEP 01 : Apply Maternity Leave

  • Select attendance and leaves and select type of leave.
  • Enter "From Date” and "To Date".
  • Enter Reason of leave and Expected date of delivery.
  • Upload Expected date of Delivery Certificate and save.

STEP 02 : Check Approval Status

  • Approval from line manager followed by approval from HRBP.
  • Wait for 24 hours post approval from L1 and HRBP.
  • Leaves shall be updated in ESS.
 Maternity hospitalization support

STEP 01 : Select Your Hospital For Cashless Support

  • Login to the Medibuddy portal. If your employee code is 55000123 then your username and password will be RGC55000123.
  • Go to Policy tab and click on “Network Hospitals” for list of empaneled hospitals.
  • Next download your DHS e-card by going to Policy -> Download E-Card.
  • During hospitalization, produce your e-card to receive cashless support.
  • If your hospital is not empaneled, you may avail reimbursement facility post childbirth.

STEP 02 : Initiate Maternity Claim

  • To avail this facility, create a claim request by clicking on the button below .
  • Upload the scanned copy of the hospital admit card, discharge summary, child’s birth certificate and medical bills in the request.
  • Print the form generated for your request.

STEP 03 : Share Documents For Processing

  • Send the form and the original documents (as mentioned in the form) to the insurance team on the below address.
  • The insurance team takes upto 30 – 45 days to update your request.
  • You can track the status of your claim through Medibuddy website or app.

Post Maternity Half Day Leaves

STEP 01 : Submit Request Form

  • You can apply for post maternity work schedule of “half day, half pay” for up to 6 months after maternity leave completion.
  • To opt for post maternity work schedule, download application form, get it signed by your L1 and submit it to your HRBP.
