Transfer In - Managed by Reliance

After we send out your request to your previous employer / RPFC Office for transfer, we request you to please follow up with them to ensure that the fund is transferred at the earliest. The normal lead time for transfer is 4 to 6 months.

Transfer In - Managed by RPFC

The Transfer In details are updated by the RPFC in your E Passbook, which can be viewed in the website of Employees Provident Fund Organization,India. The URL is http://members.epfoservices.in. In case the details are not updated within 6 months, you may raise a query in MyHR and FC&A services-Retiral team will help you in the follow up with the RPFC. This being a new facility launched by EPFO(launched from August 2012) there may be discrepancies in the opening balances, contributions etc. It is expected that the system will stabilize with the passage of time.

Transfer In - Managed by RPFC

The normal lead time for transfer is about 4 to 6 months, after we send your request for transfer to RPFC. You can raise a query in MyHR in case the details are not updated and FC&A services-Retiral team will help you in follow up with RPFC.
