Periodic Medical Examination(PME)

STEP 01 : Generate Letter

  • Download and print the PME letter for you & your spouse in your birthday month.
  • If you are above 40 years old, then you and your spouse must take PME yearly.
  • If you are less than 40 years old, then you must take test yearly and your spouse must take test once in two years.

STEP 02 : PME Examination And Claim

  • Show printout of PME letter at the hospital along with your ID card.
  • You can get PME done at any hospital, which can be reimbursed later. Mumbai based employees may opt for cashless PME at HN Hospital.
  • Post examination, send your reports to medical center.
  • Submit claim request after your reports are verified by medical center.
Life Insurance

STEP 01 : Add Nominations

  • Add family members as your nominee.
  • Select “GTLI” as benefit type and enter nominees details and Save.

STEP 02 : Claim Life Insurance

  • In case of death, your HRBP will contact your nominee(s) for claiming life insurance.
  • Nominees should keep a copy of all documents shared with HRBP.
Accident Insurance

STEP 01 : Add Nominations

  • Add family members as your nominee.
  • Select "GPA" as benefit type and enter nominees details and Save.

STEP 02 : Accident Intimation And Claim

  • To initiate claim process, please intimate about the accident.
  • Post intimation, you can claim your accident related expenses from the same screen.
  • Ensure you have attached all the relevant documents with your claim form.
Hospitalization Insurance

STEP 01 : Card Download

  • Download insurance card for yourself and dependents after 60 days of joining to cover any hospitalization longer than 24 hours.
  • You can download the Medibuddy card / Insurance card from our Medibuddy card service. Click on the below button.

STEP 02 : Select Your Hospital For Cashless Support

  • Login to the Medibuddy portal.
  • Go to Policy tab and click on “Network Hospitals” for list of empanelled hospitals.
  • During hospitalization, produce your e-card to receive cashless support.
  • If your hospital is not empaneled, you may avail reimbursement facility post hospitalization.

STEP 03 : Initiate Claim

  • To avail this facility, create a claim request by clicking on the button below .
  • Upload the scanned copy of the hospital admit card, discharge summary and medical bills in the request.
  • Print the form generated for your claim request.

STEP 04 : Share Documents For Processing

  • Send the form and the original documents (as mentioned in the form) to the insurance team on the below address.
  • The insurance team takes upto 30 – 45 days to update your request.
  • You can track the status of your claim through Medibuddy website or app.
