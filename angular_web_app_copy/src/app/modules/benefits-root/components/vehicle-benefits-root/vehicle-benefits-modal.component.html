 Company Leased Vehicle

STEP 01 : Submit request for Company Leased Vehicle

  • Select the car and get quote from the car showroom.
  • Share the quote with Leasing Vendor at RELIANCE.ORIX@orixindia.com
  • The vendor will share the details of the leasing arrangement with you.

STEP 02 : Upload request on the system

  • Post agreement with vendor, create the lease request on system.
  • Once the request is approved, check your Vehicle Lease amount in the total pay statement.
  • Orix will place the order for chosen vehicle at the showroom and will inform you.
  • Co-ordinate delivery details directly with the car showroom.
