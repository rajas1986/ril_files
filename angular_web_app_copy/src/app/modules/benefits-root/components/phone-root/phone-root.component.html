International Roaming/ISD Calling

STEP 01 : Apply for ISD Calling services

  • If your job requires you to make international calls, then you can apply for ISD calling services on your company sim.
  • Your application will go to your L1 and segment head for approval.
  • Post approval, your services would get activated within 24 hours.

STEP 02 : Apply for International Roaming Services

  • For work related international trips, you can apply for International Roaming services on your company sim.
  • Your application will go to your L1 and segment head for approval.
  • Post approval, your services would get activated from date of travel.
