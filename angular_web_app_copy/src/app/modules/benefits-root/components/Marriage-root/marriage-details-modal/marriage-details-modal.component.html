Marriage Leaves

STEP 01 : Update Your Family Details

  • To avail the marriage leaves of 5 consecutive days, add your soon-to-be spouse as a family member and upload your wedding invitation.
  • This shall be validated by your HRBP.

STEP 02 : Apply For Leaves

  • After 24 hours of validation, marriage leaves will appear in the leaves section.
  • You can apply for leaves by clicking on below button.
  • You can avail your marriage leaves of 5 consecutive days, one month before or after your wedding day.
  • The request will go to your L1 for approval.
 Marriage Loan

STEP 01 : Update Your Family Details

  • To avail marriage benefits, your spouse needs to be added as your family member.
  • Simply click on the button below and add the details of your soon to be spouse.
  • Upload your wedding invite in the attachment section.

STEP 02 : Apply For Loan

  • You can apply for a marriage loan of upto 3 months of your fixed pay by creating a loan request 4 months prior or 2 months after your wedding day.
  • Upload your wedding invite with the loan request and submit for approval from HRBP.
  • If the request is approved before 20th of the month then loan amount shall be credited with same month’s salary.
 Marriage Gift

STEP 01 : Update Family Details

  • You shall receive a Marriage gift when you add your spouse details and upload the wedding invite.
  • Marriage Gift will be disbursed along with subsequent month’s salary.
